üìö Essential Vocabulary for Band 8.5+

Power Collocations

Time & Experience:

  • gain experience - I gained valuable experience working abroad
  • make the most of - I try to make the most of every opportunity
  • spare time - In my spare time, I enjoy reading
  • first-hand experience - I had first-hand experience with this issue

Opinions & Attitudes:

  • strong belief - I have a strong belief in education
  • general consensus - There's a general consensus on this matter
  • mixed feelings - I have mixed feelings about social media
  • take into account - We should take into account different perspectives

Change & Development:

  • undergo changes - My city has undergone significant changes
  • make progress - Technology has made tremendous progress
  • raise awareness - We need to raise awareness about climate change
  • broaden horizons - Travel helps broaden your horizons

Essential Phrasal Verbs

Communication & Relationships:

  • get along with - I get along well with my colleagues
  • bring up - My parents brought me up to be respectful
  • catch up with - I often catch up with old friends
  • look up to - I really look up to my mentor

Problems & Solutions:

  • deal with - We need to deal with this challenge
  • come up with - We came up with an innovative solution
  • work out - Things will work out in the end
  • carry out - They carried out extensive research

Activities & Routines:

  • take up - I took up yoga last year
  • give up - I'll never give up on my dreams
  • keep up with - It's hard to keep up with technology
  • look forward to - I'm looking forward to the weekend
